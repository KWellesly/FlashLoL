What is FlashOnF?

FlashOnF is a League of Legends site where you can find simple-to-read guides on every champion. New to the game? These guides were meant to tell you everything you need to know in the timespan it takes to load into the game.

What can you expect in the future?

  • Fun and wacky community-built guides
  • Rating system for each guide
  • How to build team compositions
  • Challenge builds --> Submit your wins with them to be broadcasted on this site!
